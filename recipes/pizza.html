Low Calorie Pizza

Do you love pizza but hate calories? If so I have just the dish for you!

Here's what you need to make the pizza:

  • Pizza dough(we make this)
  • Ragu sauce
  • fat free mozzerela cheese
  • However many pepperoni's you like on your pizza

Now the part you want: How to make it!!

  1. To make our dough, combine 1/2 cup flour, 65g nonfat greek yogurt, and 1/4 teaspoon of yeast, roll into ball, and flatten on a pan.
  2. Roll edges of dough into the crust
  3. Add 1 serving of our sauce onto crust, then add cheese
  4. Add wanted toppings, I reccomend pepperoni
  5. Cook in oven at 450 degrees fahrenheit
  6. When done, remove and feast!
