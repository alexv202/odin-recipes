Chicken Alfredo

Arguably one of the most popular dishes on the planet, chicken alfredo is a go to dinner for many houses across the globe.

This dish is a classic in many houses, learn how to make it today!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 jar alfredo sauce, or your own sauce.

Now how to prepare the dish!

  1. Step 1 is to dice your chicken breasts and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Next, get your chicken cooking and in a seperate pot boil your pasta
  3. After the chicken is done and pasta cooked, combine and add sauce
  4. Cook until sauce is done, then eat!
